About Breeze!

Breeze is Shiv Nadar University's Annual Fest organized by it's lively student body. It celebrates the vibrancy of the campus with other students from various universities from all over India.

Enjoy Culture

Attend the rock concerts, participate in debates, dazzle the crowd with your amazing dance moves, or just dress up for your next profile photo.

Play and Compete

Prove your skills on the battleground. We have something for everyone. Which team are you on?

Level up! It's time to Play with Breeze

It’s time to unleash your inner prowess, gather gasps, dazzle the crowds and walk away with the fame. There is no talent unworthy of recognition and at Breeze ’16 you can click, write, debate, design, sing, dance, construct or program yourself to glory. The curtain will rise and the music will play, the spotlight will shine and the haze will fade. This time we take you into virtual reality, letting you delve deep into the action-packed world of video gaming. We will show you how to bring those moments in front of your screens to life. Rescue the princess with Mario, and save the world with CoD, race against speed itself with NFS. Brain and brawn combine to put you on the throne of this virtual reality.
